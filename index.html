Why We Stand

The Palestinian people have endured decades of occupation, displacement, and human rights violations. This platform exists to amplify their voices, educate the world, and mobilize support for a free and just future.

Historical Context

From the Nakba in 1948 to the present day, Palestinians have faced systemic oppression and loss of land. Understanding the roots of the conflict is essential to advocating for peace and justice.

  • 1948: The Nakba — over 700,000 Palestinians displaced
  • 1967: Occupation of Gaza and the West Bank begins
  • Today: Ongoing blockade, settlement expansion, and humanitarian crisis
